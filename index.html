What's inside

Everything a show needs

From first entry to final report — no spreadsheets, no Access, no internet required.

Cage Tickets

Print PDF tickets with exhibit number, class, exhibitor name, and a QR code. Club logo watermarked on every page.

Rapid Results Entry

Keyboard-driven flow — exhibit number, result, Enter. Chain through hundreds of entries without lifting your hands.

7 PDF Reports

Results sheet, show catalogue, prize money, address tags, exhibitor list — preview in-app before printing.

Special Winners

Assign special prizes by exhibit number. Manage the full prize list with trophy types and cash amounts.

Archives

Save named snapshots of the entire database. Restore any previous show — perfect before a season reset.

Global Search

Search exhibitors, entries, results, and special winners at once. Jump directly to any record in one click.

Workflow

From setup to prize ceremony

A typical show runs in a few clear steps — Benchabird guides you through each one.

1

Setup & Exhibitors

Enter show details, upload your club logo, and add all registered exhibitors.

2

Entries & Tickets

Add class entries, run Calculate to assign ticket numbers, then print cage tickets as PDF.

3

Results & Reports

Enter judging results, assign special winners, then generate and print all seven PDF reports.
